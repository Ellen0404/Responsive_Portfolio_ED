About Me

I’m happy you’re reading this section - my digital introducing. I love the photography and that vibe being behind the camera.  Freeze the moment before it fades, capture that emotion you have here and now – that’s my behind the camera mission – to give you these energies, so you can relive them forever!

I was creating a website for my photography business and the process fascinated  and excite me so much that I decided to go deeper. I found this whole world of creativity and logical connections, I found that logic could be very creative and beautiful , that was the perfect combination of that I was looking for. So here I am, exploring this amazing world of coding , tying my best and integrating this knowledge into my updated life!
